Getting Started

What is Squaredoor?

At Squaredoor our focus is take the hassle out of managing Vacation Rentals and to provide the best experience to our Guests whether it's a family vacation or a business trip. We take care of all aspects of managing a Vacation Rental starting with transforming a standard home into a fully functional Vacation Rental to handling Guest Check-in, Check-out, cleaning, payments and much more... We believe in keeping things real and simple to find and list accommodations that are suitable for all. Our dedicated team of experienced Independent Squaredoor certified Vacation Rental PRO’s (VR PRO's) excel to provide the highest quality services for our clients. As a guest, you will find convenient and safe accommodations around the world. As a property owner, you get maximum marketability and great returns for your listing.

What is a Vacation Rental PRO ?

Vacation Rental PRO's (VR PRO's) are a community of passionate local Independent Consultants who are there to offer insider tips and a wealth of local knowledge on each property and location. Squaredoor VR PRO's also assure that each listing contains the amenities and features as advertised, and provide step by step assistance to Property Owners and Guests, ensuring that everyone has an amazing experience listing and booking their Vacation Rentals with Squaredoor.

Property Owner

Who will approve my property on Squaredoor?

Each property listed on Squaredoor.com website will be carefully reviewed by the Squaredoor team in accordance with Squaredoor’s listing guidelines. Once approved the listing status will become an active and searchable listing on the website.

How do I list a property?

You can list a property in 5 simple steps or you can choose a certified Vacation Rental PRO (VR PRO) to assist you with your property.

If you choose to list the property yourself start by creating a Squaredoor account then click on List A Property button on the header to start listing your property. Enter the address, price per night, select property type, amenities, upload nice photos and write an up to date description about your property. Arrange the calendar schedules and set prices for weekends and holidays. Establish security deposit and cleaning fee. Once your property is approved by Squaredoor, guests can start booking it right way. After the property is approved be sure to respond to messages and reservations requests from prospective guests in a timely manner. Having good communication with guests will help you to get a good rating from guests when they are writing a review about your place and the overall booking experience. Be sure to select the payment method of how you want to get paid.

How do I manage my property on Squaredoor website?

Squaredoor provides a simple and comprehensive Dashboard to manage all your properties. Property Owners can view, manage, update, and deactivate a listing inside the Dashboard. Squaredoor provides Property Owners with many convenient tools, which make the process of managing a Vacation Rental very simple. Inside the Squaredoor Dashboard you will find many useful features, such as ability to view and print reports of all your bookings, find the records of invoices, deposits and other important information about the properties you have listed with Squaredoor. In addition to a robust set of online tools, as a Property Owner, you will have access to a local certified VR PRO that will be here to assist you by creating, updating and managing your property, and to answer any of your questions.

How much should I charge per night for my property?

You decide how much you want to charge per night for your property. To get an idea of what you can charge, we recommend that you search similar listings in your neighborhood to learn more about the local pricing. The quality of accommodations, amenities, size, number of rooms and the neighborhood in which the property is located are the important criteria, which would affect your nightly rate. Keep in mind, in order to have more bookings and attract more Guests the price per night should be comparable to other similar properties in the same area. Also, you should set different rates for weekends and holidays.

How should I write a property description?

We recommend choosing an attractive title which informs potential Guest about the location and the main highlight of your property and writing a detailed and accurate description that showcases everything your property has to offer to your potential Guests. It is important that the information about your rental is sufficient to give a prospective Guest a sense of what it would be like to stay at your property. In addition to the property details, you should describe your local area, some of the interesting things to do in the neighborhood, and any other unique features that make your place stand out from the rest. In advertising your property you must adhere to the Property Owner's Policy, and provide the Guest with clear and detailed rules as to the check-in and check-out, Security Deposit and Cancellation Policy. This information, along with good reviews, will help Guest notice your rental and put it at the top of their list.

What is a Property Owner's Policy?

When listing your property with Squaredoor, you are required to adhere to the Property Owner’s Policy. As a Property Owner, you have the ability to set many of the rules and restrictions related to the use of your property by the prospective Guest. It is important that you conduct the necessary research and seek legal advice as well as other professional advice in order to make sure that you and your property comply with all applicable laws, rules and regulations for your country, state, city and your local area and community. It is important that you treat your Guests as friends, and make sure that each of your Guests has a great experience staying in your vacation rental. If you provide a clean, safe, comfortable and enjoyable accommodation for your Guests, and inform them clearly and upfront about anything that may be important and helpful for them to know about your property, you will ensure that your Guests have great experience. In turn, your efforts will be repaid with great reviews and references.

How does the cleaning fee work?

When listing a property with Squaredoor you have the option to set a cleaning fee and charge a one time cleaning fee per service. Property Owners can offer a Guest an option to select more than one cleaning fee depending on the duration of the stay. The first cleaning fee is non-refundable once the Guest has checked-in. However, if the rental reservation was canceled prior to the check-in, the cleaning fee is refunded in full, regardless of other restrictions imposed by the Cancellation Policy.

How do I set up my payment method?

Squaredoor provides you with a simple process to choose how you want to get paid for each bookings. In the dashboard under Property Owner’s financials you can choose your prefered payout method.

When do I get paid after successful check-in?

Squaredoor payout the Property Owners the following Tuesday from the successful check in. Be sure to set up your payment method in advance.

How do I book a property on Squaredoor?

  1. Search by destination
  2. Select dates
  3. Message the VR PRO for any questions
  4. 2 Types of Bookings “Book it” and “Instant Booking”
  5. Upon Successful Booking you will receive a confirmation and Itinerary.

Start by searching by a desired destination. Once you find a property that you like select the dates that you wish and proceed with booking or by sending the VR PRO a message for your booking inquiry. Once the VR PRO receives your booking inquiry they can pre-approve your booking request or message you back. If the booking request is pre-approved by a VR PRO you will be notified to finalize the booking. After the booking is finalized you will receive a confirmation with your itinerary. Be sure to check carefully the Cancellation Policy for the property of your choice so that you are aware of your options in the event you need to change or cancel your reservation. After your check-out be sure to post a review about your accommodation and share your overall experience. Enjoy your stay with Squaredoor!

How does the check-in and check-out process work?

Squaredoor’s VR PRO’s and Property Owners establish their individual check-in and check-out rules for each property. Squaredoor believes in the importance of quick and efficient communication between all parties as a way of ensuring a successful booking experience for Guests and Property Owners. When you begin searching for properties on Sqauredoor, we encourage you to get in touch and communicate directly with Property Owners, and get additional assistance from VR PRO’s, assigned to the property of your choice if you have a question about a particular listing. You can find the contact information for Property Owner or VR PRO on the property’s page. As you move along the booking process, you will be able to work out the details of check-in and check-out, discuss the process for exchanging the keys, and other details directly with your VR PRO or Property Owner. VR PRO’S will be there every step of the way to ensure that all your questions are answered in the timely manner. Use the Squaredoor inbox to see all your email communications stored in one place, and respond directly from the inbox to make your communication process easy and smooth.

How does the Cancellation/Refund Policy works?

As a part of the listing process, the VR PRO’s and Property Owners will be required to choose a Cancellation Policy for their listings. Squaredoor provides the VR PRO’s and Property Owners with a three levels of the Cancellation Policy. There are three broad categories, which are Light, Medium and Strict. Each of these 3 categories offers a short and long-term options. If you are a Guest looking for an accommodation on Squaredoor, please be sure to review the applicable Cancellation Policy for the property of your choice.

How to cancel a booking?

To cancel your booking please find your “booking confirmation”, and click on “cancel booking” below your reservation details. Please note some vacation rental property Hosts impose additional cancellation deadlines and fees. If the Property Owner or VR PRO has imposed additional cancellation restrictions all cancellations made after the deadline will be charged based on the Property’s Cancellation Fee. If you have additional questions regarding imposed cancellation deadlines and fees please contact the VR PRO or the Property Owner directly. For detailed information on how to cancel your booking please see Cancellation and Refund Policy.

The refund for your cancellation will be applied to your original payment card. We will process your refund within 24 hours. Once we have issued your refund, it may take 3 to 7 business days for your bank or credit card company to process your refund. Please contact your bank or credit card company for information.
